About

Built on a wrench.
Kept running by trust.

What started as Ron Clark's lifelong obsession with pre-'50s mechanics has become a full-service shop that handles everything from vintage Mustangs to modern Subarus.

Chris Clark brings cutting-edge diagnostics alongside the family's legendary work ethic. They're the rare shop that can restore a 1932 Ford and troubleshoot a 2024 Toyota with equal confidence.

The Mechanics

Best of both worlds.

One mechanic who's worked on cars since before computers existed. One who speaks OBD-II natively. Together, they cover every year, every make.

R
Ron Clark
Co-Owner — Classic Specialist

Ron's expertise lives in the era of carburetors, points, and hand-fitted body panels. Pre-1950s vehicles are his domain — frame-up restorations, mechanical overhauls, and the kind of knowledge that only comes from decades of turning wrenches on cars that predate the computer age.

C
Chris Clark
Co-Owner — Modern Specialist

Chris handles the other side — modern diagnostics, emissions systems, electronic fuel injection, and hybrid platforms. He also runs the custom fabrication and pinstriping work that makes Clark Auto unique in the entire valley.
