About Weiss Dry Cleaners

Located at 627 Main Street in the heart of Alamosa, we've been serving the San Luis Valley as a family-owned dry cleaning and laundry service. We use eco-friendly processing methods and treat every garment as if it were our own.

We're an active part of this community — from ironing American flags for Centennial Park to pressing materials for Valley Health's mask drives. When you drop off your clothes, you're supporting a local business that invests back here.
