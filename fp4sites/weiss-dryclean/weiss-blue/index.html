About Us

Weiss Dry Cleaners has been serving the San Luis Valley from our location at 627 Main Street in Alamosa. We're a family-owned business that takes pride in every garment we touch.

We use eco-friendly cleaning methods — no harsh chemicals, just clean clothes and a cleaner environment. Plus, through our Jim's Formal Wear affiliation, we handle tuxedos, prom dresses, and wedding attire with the care they deserve.

We're an active part of this community: ironing American flags for Centennial Park, pressing materials for Valley Health's mask drives, and hosting donation collections. When you choose Weiss, you're choosing a neighbor.
