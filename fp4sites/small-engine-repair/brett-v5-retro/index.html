// Our Services

What We Fix

01

Fuel Systems

Carburetor cleaning, rebuilding, and tuning. Fuel line replacement and tank cleaning. If fuel isn't getting where it should, we fix it.

02

Ignition Systems

Spark plugs, coils, kill switch wiring, CDI modules. We trace the spark, find the break, and fix the electrical path.

03

Routine Maintenance

Oil changes, air filters, blade sharpening, seasonal tune-ups. Keep your equipment reliable with scheduled care.

04

2-Stroke Engines

Chainsaws, blowers, trimmers. Compression loss, piston wear, carb tuning — we know 2-stroke inside and out.

05

4-Stroke Engines

Mowers, generators, washers, pumps. Valve adjustment, oil systems, full overhauls. We do it all.

06

Diagnostics

Tell us what's wrong. We listen, test, and diagnose before we turn a wrench. You get an honest estimate, always.
