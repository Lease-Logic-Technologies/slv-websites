What We Repair

If it's got a small engine and it's not running right, we can help.

⛽

Fuel Systems

Carburetor cleaning and rebuilds, fuel line replacement, filter swaps, and tank cleaning. We find where the fuel stops flowing.

⚡

Ignition Systems

Spark plugs, coils, kill switches, CDI modules. We trace the spark to the source and fix the electrical path.

🛠️

Routine Maintenance

Oil changes, air filters, blade sharpening, seasonal tune-ups. Scheduled maintenance means fewer breakdowns.

🪚

2-Stroke Engines

Chainsaws, leaf blowers, weed eaters. We know compression loss, piston wear, and fuel mix problems.

🌾

4-Stroke Engines

Mowers, generators, pressure washers. Full service from valve adjustment to complete overhauls.

🔍

Diagnostics

Won't start? Runs rough? We diagnose first, then fix. You get an estimate before any work begins — no surprises.
