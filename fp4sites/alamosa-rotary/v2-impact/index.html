The Four-Way Test

A moral code used by Rotarians worldwide for personal and business relationships.

Question One

Is it the TRUTH?

Of the things we think, say, or do — we ask whether it is true.

Question Two

Is it FAIR to all?

We consider whether our actions are fair to everyone involved.

Question Three

Will it build GOODWILL?

We strive to create goodwill and strengthen friendships.

Question Four

Will it be BENEFICIAL?

We ensure our work benefits all concerned, not just a few.
