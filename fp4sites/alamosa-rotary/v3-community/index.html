What Is Rotary?

Rotary is the oldest and largest service club in the world. Founded in 1905 by Paul Harris, it grew from a group of four Chicago businessmen into a global network of 1.2 million people in more than 200 countries.

The Alamosa Rotary Club is part of District 5040 and Rotary International. We're a diverse group of professional leaders who share a commitment to Service Above Self — both right here in the San Luis Valley and around the world.

Whether it's supporting local youth programs, funding clean water projects internationally, or contributing to polio eradication, we work together to make things better.
