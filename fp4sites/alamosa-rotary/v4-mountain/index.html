Our Purpose

Service Above Self

From local community projects to global polio eradication, Rotary members turn fellowship into action.

Supporting Alamosa and the SLV through community programs, youth initiatives, and local partnerships.

🌍

Our Guiding Code

The Four-Way Test

I

Is it the TRUTH?

We ask first whether what we think, say, or do is true.

II

Is it FAIR?

Whether our actions are fair to all those concerned.

III

Will it build GOODWILL?

Whether it will build goodwill and better friendships.

IV

Will it be BENEFICIAL?

Whether it will be beneficial to all concerned.
