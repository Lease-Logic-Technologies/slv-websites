Born From the Earth

🌋 The Discovery

In 1955, the founders of Splashland were drilling for oil on the site and struck an unexpected geothermal pocket — hot water flowing at 104°F straight from the earth. They turned a drilling accident into a family legacy that endures to this day.

🌡️ Nature Heats the Pool

The geothermal water enters the pool at a constant 104°F year-round. Temperatures average 88–96°F across the pool — naturally heated, no boilers needed. It's the San Luis Valley's hidden geothermal treasure.
