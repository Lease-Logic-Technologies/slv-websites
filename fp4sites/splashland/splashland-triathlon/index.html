Splashland Benefit Sprint Triathlon — Alamosa, Colorado

The Race

Sprint Triathlon — Run • Bike • Swim
3.4 mi + 10 mi + 400 yd

A brutal, beautiful sprint through the heart of the San Luis Valley. Run the flat Colorado plains, bike through scenic high desert, and cap it off with a lap in 104° geothermal water. No other triathlon in Colorado finishes in a hot spring.

\u23f1\ Run — 3.4 Miles

Flat, fast course through Alamosa and the Valley. Early morning, cool air, big sky. The route winds through city streets and trails before heading back to Splashland for the swim.

\ud83d\udeb4\ Bike — 10 Miles

Rolling high desert terrain with sweeping views of the Sangre de Cristo Mountains. A beautiful, challenging loop through the Valley before the final swim leg.
