Why Splashland?

Everything you need in a family pool, plus one thing you can't find anywhere else in Colorado.

The Discovery

Drilling for Oil. Finding Magic.

In 1955, the founders of Splashland struck a geothermal pocket while drilling for oil. Hot water flowed at 104\°F straight from the earth. They turned a drilling accident into one of Colorado's most beloved family destinations.

1955 — now
Natural Heat

Natural geothermal water enters the pool at 104\°F year-round. Average pool temperature: 88–96\°F. No electricity. No gas. The mountain heats this pool.
