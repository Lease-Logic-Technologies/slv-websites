// Capabilities

Repair & Maintenance

From routine tune-ups to complete engine rebuilds. We handle the full spectrum of small engine service.

Fuel Systems

Carburetor cleaning, rebuilding, and tuning. Fuel line replacement, filter swaps, and tank cleaning. We diagnose lean and rich conditions so your engine runs clean.

Carburetor

Ignition Systems

Spark plug diagnostics and replacement, coil testing, kill switch wiring, and CDI module repair. We trace the electrical path to find the fault.

Ignition

Routine Maintenance

Oil changes, air filter replacement, blade sharpening, and seasonal pre-season tune-ups. Keep your equipment running between repairs with scheduled maintenance.

Maintenance

2-Stroke Engines

Chainsaws, leaf blowers, trimmers, and more. We diagnose compression loss, piston wear, and carburetor issues specific to 2-stroke power units.

2-Stroke

4-Stroke Engines

Lawn mowers, generators, pressure washers, and pumps. Full engine overhaul, valve adjustment, and oil system repair for dependable 4-stroke performance.

4-Stroke
