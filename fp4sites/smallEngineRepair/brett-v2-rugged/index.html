What We Fix

From carburetor cleanings to full engine rebuilds — if it's got a small engine, we handle it.

⛽

Fuel Systems

Carburetor cleaning, rebuild, and tuning. Fuel lines, filters, and tank issues. If it won't run right on gas, we find the problem.

⚡

Ignition Systems

Spark plugs, coils, kill switches, and CDI modules. Electrical gremlins traced and fixed so you start reliably every time.

🔧

Routine Maintenance

Oil changes, air filters, blade sharpening, seasonal tune-ups. Keep your equipment reliable between repairs.

🪚

2-Stroke Engines

Chainsaws, blowers, trimmers. We know the unique needs of 2-stroke power — compression, piston wear, mix ratios.

🌿

4-Stroke Engines

Mowers, generators, washers, pumps. Full service including valve adjustment, oil systems, and complete overhauls.

🔍

Diagnostics

Won't start? Runs rough? Overheating? We diagnose before we touch anything. Call for an estimate — always upfront pricing.
