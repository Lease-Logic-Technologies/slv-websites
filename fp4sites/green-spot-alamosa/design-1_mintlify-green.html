What We Carry

Everything for Your Garden

From indoor houseplants to full-season outdoor gardening, we stock everything the San Luis Valley gardener needs.

Houseplants

Indoor greenery for every light condition. From succulents to large statement plants, find your perfect indoor companion.

Annuals & Perennials

Season-blooming annuals and hardy perennials that thrive in the high-altitude San Luis Valley climate.

Trees

Ornamental and fruit trees selected for the valley's short growing season. Expert planting advice included.

Vegetable Plugs

Start your home garden with quality vegetable transplants. Seasonal varieties suited for Colorado gardening.

Water-Wise Solutions

Native plants, drought-tolerant species, and expert guidance on water-conscious landscaping for the valley.

Potting soil, mulch, fertilizers, garden tools, and everything else to keep your garden thriving all season.

Antiques & Kitchen Store
