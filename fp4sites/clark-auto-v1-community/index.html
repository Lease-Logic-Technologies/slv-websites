Our Story

Two generations of Clark mechanics

Clark Auto Service has been serving the San Luis Valley since 2003. Founded by Ron Clark, a specialist in classic cars and pre-1950s vehicles, the shop has grown under his son Chris Clark's leadership to handle everything from vintage restorations to the latest hybrid and electric vehicle diagnostics.

We believe in honest, transparent service. We'll always show you what's needed, explain the cost before starting work, and never recommend repairs that aren't necessary. That's why our customers keep coming back.
