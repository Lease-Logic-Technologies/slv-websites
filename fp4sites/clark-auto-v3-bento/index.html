About Us
The Clark family story

Two generations of mechanics, one trusted shop

Clark Auto Service has been a staple of the Alamosa community since 2003. What started as Ron Clark's passion for classic cars has grown into a full-service auto repair shop that handles everything from vintage restorations to the latest hybrid vehicles.

Chris Clark, Ron's son, brings modern diagnostic expertise and advanced technical training to complement the family's deep mechanical knowledge. Together, they offer a rare combination of old-school craftsmanship and cutting-edge technology.

Our philosophy is simple: do the right thing for the customer. We show you what's needed, explain it clearly, and never recommend repairs that aren't necessary. That's how we've built our reputation.
