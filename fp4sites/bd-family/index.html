Our Story

B & D's Laundromat is family-owned and operated from 510 La Veta Avenue in Alamosa. The owners live on-site, which means we're always just a step away if you need help finding a machine, getting quarters, or just need someone to chat with while your load runs.

We've built this place on hard work, honesty, and treating every customer the way we'd want to be treated. It's not fancy — it's family.
