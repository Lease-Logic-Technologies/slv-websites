About

A family shop built on trust

Learn about the people behind the wrenches.

Clark Auto Service has been serving Alamosa and the surrounding San Luis Valley communities since 2003. Founded by Ron Clark, a specialist in classic cars and pre-1950s vehicles, the shop has grown into a full-service repair facility under the leadership of his son, Chris Clark.

We believe in doing the right thing for our customers. That means showing you what needs attention, explaining the cost before starting any work, and never recommending something you don't need.

  • Family-owned since 2003
  • ASE-certified technicians
  • Honest, transparent pricing
  • 24-month warranty on parts and labor
  • All makes and models serviced
  • Classic car specialists
