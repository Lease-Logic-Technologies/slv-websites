Upcoming Events

🎉

Monthly — Usually 3rd Tuesday

Monthly Business & Education Meeting

Join fellow conservatives each month for business meetings, education, and community. Meetings are usually held on the 3rd Tuesday of the month at 5:30 PM at Juanito’s in Alamosa.

⚖

Annual — Spring

Alamosa County GOP County Caucus

The annual County Caucus is where party members gather to elect delegates to the County Assembly, adopt a county platform, and organize for the year ahead. Details will be posted as the date approaches.

🏛

Annual — Spring

County Assembly

The County Assembly delegates from each precinct gather to elect delegates to the State Assembly. Contact Gary Goodwin (719-588-5011) for details.

🍽

Annual — February

Lincoln Day Dinner

Our signature annual event celebrating Abraham Lincoln’s birthday with dinner, keynote speakers, and fellow conservatives. Held at the Ski Hi Complex in Monte Vista. VIP reception and general admission tickets available.
