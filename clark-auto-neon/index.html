About Us

Built in the Valley.
For the Valley.

Clark Auto Service is a family-owned auto repair shop on Tremont Street in Alamosa, Colorado. What started as Ron Clark tinkering in a garage has grown into a full-service shop — but the philosophy hasn't changed: treat every car like it's your own, and never charge for work you wouldn't do on your own vehicle.

Today, father and son Ron and Chris Clark keep that tradition alive — blending two generations of hands-on mechanical expertise with modern diagnostic equipment.
